About IdleCare


For years, do-it-yourself consumers have had online resources available to help with buying car parts, but not until IdleCare has there been a resource dedicated to helping consumers with the very basics of car maintanance and where to purchase the exact part for their make and model. Our company has brought together all the information and resources needed to make automobile ownership an easy and personalized experience. We use the VIN from your vehicle to retrieve ONLY the most critical information that can be of use to the do-it-yourselfer. With that data we can be sure that you find exactly what you need and we direct you to an affordable option where to purchase. We help avoid searching for things that are normally hard to locate like: Tire pressure, type of oil in your car, battery part number, and light bulb replacement. We store the data specific to you in your profile and the information is easily accesible.

About VINs


Vehicle identification numbers (VINs) were first used by auto manufacturers in 1954. Between 1954 and 1981, there wasn't a standard format and VINs varied considerably between manufacturers. Under standards set by the National Highway Transportation and Safety Administration (NHTSA), every car and light truck model year 1981 or later has a unique 17-digit VIN in a fixed format.
